Campus life.

Junior school building.

Was built in 2019.

The junior school building has 16 classrooms, three for kindagarten, three for year one, three for year two, three for year three, three for year four and one for after school care.


Library

Was built a long time ago.

The library has a lot of books and places to sit and read quietly, there are also study rooms, classrooms, staffrooms and digital tech support in this one building.


Reception

Was built a long time ago.

The reception has two parts, one for the parents and one for the student, the student reception includes the medicine bay. The reception includes staffroom.


Space

Was built in 2020

The space has a gym. But if you go upstairs, there are classrooms with movable walls, so it appears to have 5 classrooms, but when the walls are moved, it acts like a stadium.
